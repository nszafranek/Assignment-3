Pizza Dough

Ingredients

Starter

  • Baking Yeast1 tbsp
  • Warm Water 1.5 cups
  • Flour 3 tbsp
  • Sugar 1 tbsp

Wet Ingredients

  • Olive oil1 tbsp
  • Garlic1 tsp
  • Rice Vinegar0.5 tsp Rice Vinegar and Nutritional Yeast are used in this recipe to add a more fermented flavour in less time.

Dry Ingredients

  • Flour470 gr
  • Oregano0.5 tsp
  • Basil0.5 tsp
  • Nutritional Yeast30 gr
    • Rice Vinegar and Nutritional Yeast are used in this recipe to add a more fermented flavour in less time.
  • Salt1 tsp

Directions

  1. Place yeast, starter flour and sugar in large bowl.
  2. Pour in 1.5 cups warm water.
  3. Mix ingredients to ensure activation.
  4. Cover bowl and let stand for at least 5 minutes.
  5. Measure out and combine dry ingredients in another large bowl.
  6. Add wet ingredients.
  7. Mix in dry ingredients until the dough comes together.
  8. Knead/Mix a few more minutes after the dough takes shape.
  9. Place dough on greased sheet pan. Grease top of dough and cover in plastic wrap for 20 minutes.
  10. Preheat oven to 220 C°/435 F°.
  11. After raising spread the dough to edges with well oiled hands, trying not to rip the dough or push out the air.
  12. Top with your preferred sauce and toppings.
  13. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden.
  14. Slice and Serve
